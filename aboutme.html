About

Hello, I'm Laura. Born and raised in southeast Michigan, I live with my spouse, 2 daughters, 2 cats and our dog. I feel so fortunate to obtain my Master of Library Science and begin a career in the informational field. I am passionate about continuing this adventure as I love to read, learn, create, and help others. Aside from my passion in books and reading, I'd say I have an "old soul" as I love anything vintage, history, museums, and architecture. My favorite things to do for fun is spend time with my family, travel, bike rides, concerts, and festivals.
